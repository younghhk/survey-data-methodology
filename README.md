# Survey Data Methodology

Methods and applied examples for analyzing complex survey data in population health and cancer epidemiology, with a focus on design-based inference and real-world datasets such as NHANES.

## Topics covered

- Complex survey design and weighting
- Measurement error and validation studies
- Small area estimation and sparse data


Each subdirectory contains applied examples and short methodological notes.
